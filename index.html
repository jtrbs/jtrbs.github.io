CS240 Project1

By Tairan Jin

In this project, I use XQuery to write queries on v-emps.xml and v-depts.xml to solve following problems. I use baseX to develop and test. Also I have solved two problems with extra credits successfully.

Note: All the results are visualized with stylesheets for Ectra Credit!

Problem1

Selection and Temporal Projection. Retrieve the employment history of employee "Anneke Preusig" (i.e., the departments where she worked and the periods during which she worked there)
Solution: The query is Query1.xq and the result is Q1_result visualized with stylesheets.

Problem2

Temporal Snapshot. Retrieve the name, salary and department of each employee who, on 1995-01-06 was making less than $44000.
Solution: You can see the query Query2.xq and the result is Q2_result visualized with stylesheets.

Problem3

Temporal Slicing. For all departments, show their history in the period starting on 1994-05-01 and ending 1996-05-06.
Solution: The query is Query3.xq and the result is Q3_result visualized with stylesheets.

Problem4

Duration: For each employee, show the longest period during which he/she went with no change in salary and his/her salary during that time.
Solution: You can see the query Query4.xq and the result is Q4_result visualized with stylesheets.

Problem5

Temporal Join. For each employee show title history and his/her manager history
Solution: The query is Query5.xq and the result is Q5_result visualized with stylesheets.

Problem6

Temporal Count. Print the history of employee count for (i) each department, and (ii) the whole company.
(i) Solution: The query is Query6_1.xq and the result is Q6_1_result visualized with stylesheets.
(ii) Solution: The query is Query6_2.xq and the result is Q6_2_result visualized with stylesheets.

Problem7

Temporal Max. For the employees in department d005, find the maximum of their salaries over time, and print the history of such a maximum.
Solution: The query is Query7.xq and the result is Q7_result visualized with stylesheets.
